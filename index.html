Memorable Activities & Celebrations

Create lasting memories with these special Mother's Day activities.

Breakfast in Bed

Start mom's special day with a delicious breakfast served right in bed. It's a classic way to show your appreciation and kick off a day of celebration.

Quick Tips:

  • Plan the menu the day before
  • Set up a tray with a small vase of flowers
  • Include her favorite morning beverage
  • Add a handwritten note for a personal touch

Movie Marathon

Create the perfect movie marathon featuring mom's favorite films. Make it extra special with comfortable seating, plenty of snacks, and uninterrupted quality time.

Quick Tips:

  • Select 3-4 of her favorite movies ahead of time
  • Create a cozy viewing area with pillows and blankets
  • Prepare a variety of snacks and treats
  • Turn off all distractions and focus on enjoying the movies together

Garden Visit & Picnic

Take mom to a beautiful botanical garden or park and enjoy a peaceful day surrounded by nature. Pack a delicious picnic to make the day even more special.

Quick Tips:

  • Check local gardens for any special Mother's Day events
  • Pack a comfortable blanket and cushions
  • Prepare a picnic with mom's favorite foods
  • Bring a camera to capture the beautiful moments

Mom's Day 101: Origin & Celebration

Mother's Day is celebrated on the second Sunday in May in many countries around the world. The modern holiday was first celebrated in 1908 when Anna Jarvis held a memorial for her mother at St Andrew's Methodist Church in Grafton, West Virginia.

Anna Jarvis wanted to honor her mother by continuing the work she started and to set aside a day to honor all mothers because she believed a mother is "the person who has done more for you than anyone in the world".

Today, Mother's Day is celebrated in various ways worldwide. It has become one of the biggest holidays for consumer spending, with people buying flowers, cards, jewelry, and special meals to celebrate the maternal figures in their lives.
